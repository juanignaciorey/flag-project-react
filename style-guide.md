- Dark Blue (Dark Mode: Elements): hsl(209, 23%, 22%)
- Very Dark Blue (Dark Mode Background): hsl(207, 26%, 17%)
- Very Dark Blue (Light Mode Background): hsl(200, 15%, 8%)
- Very Gray (Light Mode Input): hsl(0, 0%, 52%)
